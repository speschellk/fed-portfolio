Kay Christensen is a musician, artist, builder, tech aficionada, and aspiring developer.

In a prevous career, she spent nine years in the test prep industry, managing people, programs, and training teams, and teaching people how to ace grad-level entrance tests. This experience imbued her with a love of test architecture and people alike. As for education, Kay went to two great schools--University of Pennsylvania and Stanford University--and concentrated in things that people don't usually do there--music and learning, design and technology, respectively.

Kay uses her spare time to noodle around in code, enjoy great bars and sushi restaurants, build structures for her frontyard chickens, play in her house band, and knit robot monsters for children she knows.
